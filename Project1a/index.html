Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the theory and development of computer systems that perform tasks that resembles humans. Some of these actions include visual perception, speech recognition, decision making, task completion, and translation between languages and human emotions. As a result, labor costs are decreased and prices on many different products and services have decreased as well. However, this has resulted in a new job market that requires computer science and analytical skills from humans in order to operate these machines. Brain Computer Interface

Published March 7, 2017 at 12:38 pm

A brain-computer interface (BCI) is a direct communication pathway between an enhanced or wired brain and an external device. This article is about a study performed by a group of scientists and neuroengineers at the Ecole Polytechnique Federale Lausanne in Switzerland. The team consisted of scientists from the United States, Switzerland, and Germany. Together, they were able to use BCI to bypass spinal cord injuries in a pair of rhesus macaques for one of their patients.

       As a result, the man had restored intentional leg movement in his leg which previously was permanently paralyzed. The researchers shared that this was the first time a neural prosthetic has been implemented within a human. Currently, this advancement in neurology, computer science, and engineering has opened the doors for opportunities to further advance research in BCI. User Experience

Published March 7, 2017 at 12:38 pm

User experience can be described as the experience a person has while using a particular product and, generally speaking, how easy or pleasing it is to use. A recent study has shown that withint the last 5 years, S&P companies such as coca-cola, ford, and Walt Disney have outperformed other industries by 228%. One of the factors that play a role in the success of these companies is because they are able to see design as much more than just an aesthetic.  

       For instance, if you had to use a manual to use the iPhone for the first time, this would not be a good design, no matter how beautiful the phone looks, or how nice it feels in your hand. When a product is not user-friendly or creates a bad user experience, that equals a fundamentally flawed or “bad” design. Another factor that all of these companies have in common is that they focus on selling experiences rather that products. This is achieved by thinking of the customer through every phase of development and even thinking of different personas of potential customers. Next, these companies often exceed the expectations of their users making "delight" a number one KPI. Furthermore, these companies make user experience the company philosophy, so not only are the developers/engineers/designers thinking of the customers through every phase of development, but also the business analysts, customer service representatives, ad other types of employees.

Internet of Things

Published March 7, 2017 at 12:38 pm

The internet of things can be described as the internet of computing devices embedded in everyday objects, enabling them to send or receive data. Additionally, if one thing can prevent the internet of things transforming the way we live and word, it would be because of a breakdown in security. This article shares an interesting perspective on how the internet of things are far bigger than majority of people even realize.

       For example, in 2007 there was a bridge collapse in my home state in Minnesota. As a result of the steel plates inadequately supporting the bridge's load, it eventually collapsed killing many people. Through the use of the internet of things, the state of minnesota has utilized technology in order to retrieve data about the quality of bridges by using smart cement. If there’s ice on the bridge, the same sensors in the concrete will detect it and communicate the information via the wireless internet to your car. Once your car knows there’s a hazard ahead, it will instruct the driver to slow down, and if the driver doesn’t, then the car will slow down for him.

       Of all the technology trends that are taking place right now, perhaps the biggest one is the Internet of Things. It is s the only technology that’s going to give us the most disruption, as well as opportunities in the future of the human race.
